About Me


From large public companies (such as U.S. Bank and Korn Ferry), private start-ups, to the U.S. Government, I’ve enhanced my skills in product and project management, financial analysis, brand strategy, go-to-market, and business strategy implementation. Over the years, my career as taken me to experience industries such as consulting, hospitality, technology, food and agriculture, venture capital, and financial services.


Developing my tireless work ethic within the U.S. Army and my knowledge at the Carlson school of Management, I’ve helped numerous founders commercialize their ideas while also understanding the “other side of the table” of financing entrepreneurial ventures, assisting in deal flow, due diligence, and investor relations.


Passionate about innovation and entrepreneurship, I am driven to help founders find success and bring new ideas into the world.
