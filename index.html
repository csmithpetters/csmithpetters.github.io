About Me

I am a skilled people, project, and technical manager with a passion for tech, traveling to new places, and trying unique and local foods. Delivering a balanced blend of people and technical skills, I bring a diverse mindset to any team or organization that I am a part of.

As data-driven as I am, I always remember that behind every decision are people. From public companies, private start-ups, to the U.S. Military, I’ve grown my skills in team development, leadership, project management, and business strategy implementation.

My specialties include on-the-spot problem solving, analytical thinking, and learning new skills. This includes web development and programming languages, and I’m enthusiastic about learning as many other languages, frameworks, or general principles that I can.

I also enjoy meeting new people and hearing about new perspectives so please feel free to reach out to me to talk about any new emerging tech, entrepreneurship, or DIY projects.

When I’m not managing projects, you can usually find me outside with my family, listening to podcasts, daydreaming about new places to go, learning a new programming language, or playing games on my computer.
